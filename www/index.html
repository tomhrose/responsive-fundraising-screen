Thank you for supporting the Clarks Summit Fire Company. Your dollars help support your community.

Some interesting facts about the Clarks Summit Fire Company:

  • We proudly serve Clarks Summit, Clarks Green, Glenburn, Waverly, Abington and parts of South Abington.
  • Fire company membership is open to those ages 14 and older. Contributory and Auxiliary memberships are also available.
  • Every year in October during National Fire Prevention Week we visit schools in our area to educate the children on fire safety.
  • Our Sunday Breakfast buffet is held on the third Sunday of every month from 8:00am to 12:00pm.
  • Our banquet hall holds up to 120 people and is available for rent. Call 570.586.9656 for details.
  • You can follow us on Facebook
